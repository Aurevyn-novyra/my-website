About Me

I am Priyanshu Sharma, the voice behind Aurevyn Novyra. My words are my sanctuary, a library built of echoes, dreams, and stories waiting to be heard. Each piece is a doorway to another soul, a candle flickering in the silent halls of poetry. Here, you will find fragments of me scattered like forgotten pages of a timeless book.
